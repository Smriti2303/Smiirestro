At PARTHRESTRO, we believe in serving food that brings people together. Our restaurant combines modern flavors with traditional techniques to create a unique dining experience.

Our Story


Founded in 2015, PARTHRESTRO was built on the love of food and community. Our founder, Chef Parth, wanted to create a space where people could enjoy innovative cuisine in a warm and inviting atmosphere.


Since then, we have grown into a landmark in the city, known for our creative dishes and excellent service. From seasonal ingredients to specialty cocktails, we always strive to offer something new and exciting.

PARTHRESTRO began as a dream shared by Chef Parth and his close-knit team of culinary enthusiasts. What started as a small pop-up kitchen in 2015 quickly grew into a full-fledged restaurant with a distinct identity. Our vision was to create a place where people could enjoy innovative cuisine without the pretension often associated with fine dining. In our early days, we traveled across the country, exploring different food cultures and sourcing unique ingredients. This journey inspired our menu, which reflects a fusion of global flavors with a local touch. Our commitment to using fresh, seasonal produce not only ensures high-quality dishes but also supports local farmers and reduces our carbon footprint. As we grew, our focus on sustainability and community engagement became a cornerstone of our ethos. We implemented waste reduction practices and launched programs to support local charities and food banks. Our "Give Back Nights" offer a portion of our proceeds to these community causes, reinforcing our commitment to making a positive impact beyond our kitchen. Today, PARTHRESTRO has become a landmark in the city, known for its creative dishes, exceptional service, and welcoming atmosphere. Whether you're here for a romantic dinner, a family celebration, or a casual lunch, we aim to make every visit memorable.


Our Values


  • Quality Ingredients: We believe that great food starts with the finest ingredients. That's why we partner with local farmers, artisanal producers, and sustainable suppliers to source our produce, meat, and seafood. Our chefs are passionate about creating dishes that highlight these high-quality components, resulting in a dining experience that's as flavorful as it is memorable.

  • Exceptional Service:At PARTHRESTRO, we understand that dining out is about more than just food; it's about the entire experience. Our team is dedicated to providing attentive, personalized service that makes you feel at home. From the moment you step through our doors, we want you to feel welcomed, valued, and cared for. We train our staff to anticipate your needs and make every visit special.

  • Community Engagement: We believe in giving back to the community that has supported us from the start. Our "Give Back Nights" and other charitable initiatives are designed to make a tangible difference. We also host events that bring people together, like cooking classes, farm-to-table dinners, and wine tastings. These events not only strengthen our community ties but also create lasting memories.

  • Sustainability:Sustainability is at the heart of our operations. We strive to minimize our environmental impact through waste reduction, recycling programs, and energy-efficient practices. Our menu changes seasonally to reduce food waste, and we compost organic material to nourish local gardens. By focusing on sustainability, we aim to create a better future for our planet and the generations to come.
